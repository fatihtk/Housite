    Know Us Better

    Property Mavens is Melbourne’s leading independent property advisory firm. Offering award winning buyer advocacy and property investment advice services to time poor professionals, anxious investors and frustrated home buyers in need of timely, professional and profitable property outcomes.

    We provide a range of services including researching, sourcing, assessing and acquiring property both off and pre market via our well established agent networks. At Property Mavens you can be assured the information you are given is based on decades of experience and extensive research to ensure we only recommend high performing investment grade properties. An experienced real estate agency, Property Mavens provides you with the unbiased and independent evidence you need, to support your buying decisions. Applying well-honed analytical and negotiation skills for investors, our client’s earn up to 40% capital growth per annum. We do not sell or recommend new or off the plan properties to our clients and we provide genuine independent and licensed buyer advocacy solutions. Applying multiple skill sets for homebuyers, we help you to select the right property, for the right price and we protect clients from overpaying. Because your home is your biggest asset, we also ensure your property has strong underlying investment fundamentals. We also provide Property Portfolio Reviews and select Vendor Advocacy services.
